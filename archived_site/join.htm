Join CUPLC

The club is friendly, open and encourages new members, regardless of previous experience. Don't be worried or intimidated because you're not the strongest in the club; we all remember our first steps, and we're all very happy to help anyone just starting out with training and technique questions (make sure to ask!).

Membership is £ 25 a year. Benefits to being a club member include:

  • Advice on technique, programming and diet
  • Lifting seminars (for beginner and intermediate lifters)
  • Use of specialised equipment (such as Eleiko bars and plates in the Cambridge Sports Centre)
  • Free physio appointments
  • Discount for attending competitions
  • Club stash
  • Exclusive socials
  • Access to the club Facebook page
  • Possibility to attend Powerlifting Varsity
